squidback.xyz

web-based Larsen generator

Most spaces and audio system have resonant frequencies: Larsen effect is a continuous reinforcement of these frequencies, that makes them (very) audible. As such, it is reactive to the distance between speakers and microphones, to the architecture of the place where the system is situated, and to bodies inhabiting it. Squidback is a self-regulating system, that strives to generate a variety of feedback tones, while staying reactive to its environment.

Feedbacks can be loud, check your volume.
